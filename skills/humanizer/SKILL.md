# Humanizer

Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text.

## What it detects and fixes
- Inflated symbolism
- Promotional language
- Vague attributions
- Em dash overuse
- Rule of three patterns
- AI vocabulary words ("delve", "crucial", "landscape")
- Excessive conjunctive phrases

Based on Wikipedia's comprehensive "Signs of AI writing" guide.

## Usage
Read this SKILL.md and apply its principles when editing text.
